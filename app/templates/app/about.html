ABOUT US

Fashion is most often thought of a global industry that is invested in anticipating what we wear and how we wish to appear to others. But fashion isn't just a business. It's also a cultural and social phenomenon, driven by the desire for the new. As such, the industry can never fully control fashion: fashion is all about being open to change.
